Controlled Vocabulary
Online Bibliography of Southeastern Zooarchaeology
Best Practices for tagging: A minimum of three (3) tags is required for each entry,
with a maximum of five (5) total. All tags should come from the OBSZ Controlled
Vocabulary. If a work requires a tag that is not on this list, it must first be approved
by the Project Manager. The preferred order of tag categories follows the listing
here. Tags should be used above and beyond terms given in the references title,
abstract, or author-provided keywords. For example,
Austin, Robert J., Lisabeth Carlson, and Richard W. Estabrook
2009 Archaic Period Faunal Use in the West-Central Florida Interior. Southeastern
Archaeology 28(2): 148-164.
APPROPRIATE TAGS: pit, post, vertebrates, inland
